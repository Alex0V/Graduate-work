Art of meditation

It is a theme that combines the art of meditation with the desire for peace, freedom and connection with nature.
Calmness
Calmness refers to a state of tranquility, serenity, and inner peace. It is a quality of being mentally and emotionally composed, free from agitation, stress, or excessive excitement. Cultivating calmness is essential for overall well-being and can contribute to a more balanced and fulfilling life.
Freedom
Freedom in the context of the art of meditation means a sense of inner freedom from the limitations of mind and emotions. It can be a feeling of detachment from internal obstacles, stereotypes, stress and negative thought patterns.
Nature
Nature is one of the most important sources of inspiration for the art of meditation. Connecting with nature can give us a sense of peace, presence and harmony. The open space of nature, the greenness of trees, the rustling of leaves or the calmness of water can serve as an object of awareness during meditation.
